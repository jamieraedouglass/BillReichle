Common Questions
Do you work outside of the North Shore? +

Our primary practice area is the North Shore of Chicago—Lake County through Evanston. We consider projects beyond that area on a case-by-case basis. Reach out and let us know where you're located.

How do architectural fees work? +

We typically work on a percentage of construction cost for full-service commissions, and hourly for consulting or partial services. We're transparent about fees from the first conversation—no surprises.

What's the first step? +

Fill out the form on this page and we'll schedule a brief introductory call. We'll ask questions about your project, answer yours about our process, and determine whether we're a good fit.

How long does an architectural project take? +

A new residential project typically takes 12–18 months from first meeting through permit issuance, with construction adding another 12–24 months. Renovations and furniture projects move faster. We'll give you a realistic timeline at our first meeting.
