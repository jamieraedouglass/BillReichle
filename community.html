Interested in partnering with Bill?

Bill is deeply committed to philanthropy and is always seeking new opportunities to make a meaningful impact. His dedication to giving back is a core part of his personal and professional values. He believes that strong communities are built through shared effort and investment, and he is eager to learn about organizations that are making a real difference.

If you represent a non-profit and believe your mission aligns with Bill's philanthropic goals, he encourages you to reach out. He is particularly interested in initiatives focused on education, environmental conservation, and healthcare access. Bill is not just a donor; he is a partner who wants to understand the challenges you face and the vision you have for the future. He is committed to building lasting relationships with the non-profits he supports, offering not only financial contributions but also his time, expertise, and network.

Please contact his office to schedule a time to discuss your organization's work and how a partnership with Bill could help you achieve your goals. He looks forward to hearing from you and learning about the incredible work you do.
