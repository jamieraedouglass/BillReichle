Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Bill Reichle Counseling / Avanti Consulting LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or contact us about our services.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit an appointment request or contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and a description of what you are seeking help with. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and schedule appointments.

Automatically collected information

We may collect standard website usage data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and referring pages through tools like Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and anonymous and is used only to improve the website experience.

How we use your information

  • To respond to appointment requests and inquiries
  • To schedule and manage counseling sessions
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Confidentiality and HIPAA

Bill Reichle Counseling takes your privacy seriously. Information shared in counseling sessions is protected under federal and state confidentiality laws, including HIPAA where applicable. Information submitted through this website contact form is not considered protected health information (PHI) until a formal therapeutic relationship is established.

Any information shared in a clinical context will be handled in accordance with our Notice of Privacy Practices, which will be provided at the start of services.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and collect anonymous usage statistics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics for website analytics and Square for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies and data practices. We encourage you to review their policies if you have concerns.

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information submitted through this website. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly at Bill@BillReichle.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
