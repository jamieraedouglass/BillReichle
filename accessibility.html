Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 2026

Bill Reichle Counseling / Avanti Consulting LLC is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including people with disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and services.

Our commitment

We are actively working to improve the accessibility of this website in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines help make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

What we have done

We have taken the following steps to improve accessibility on this site:

  • Used semantic HTML elements to support screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Added descriptive alt text to all meaningful images
  • Ensured sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Made interactive elements (buttons, links, forms) keyboard-navigable
  • Used ARIA labels and roles where appropriate
  • Designed a mobile-responsive layout that works across devices and screen sizes
  • Used clear, plain language throughout the site

Known limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas of the site may still have limitations. We are actively working to address these and welcome feedback to help us improve.

Alternative access

If you are unable to access any content or functionality on this website due to a disability, we are happy to provide the information in an alternative format. Please contact us and we will do our best to assist you promptly.

Physical office accessibility

Our West Chester office at 1390 Birmingham Road, Suite 303 is located on an upper floor. If you have mobility or accessibility needs related to in-person visits, please contact us in advance so we can discuss the best way to accommodate you, including virtual session options.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know.
