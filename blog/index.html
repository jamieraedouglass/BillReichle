Our Story

A Speakeasy Worth Finding

Caruso's wasn't built — it was collected. Bottle by bottle, glass by glass, over years of hunting for the right spirits, the right atmosphere, and the right crowd. Tucked away in Lake Bluff, it's the kind of place you have to know about to find.

This is a home bar in the truest sense: personal, carefully curated, and open to those who appreciate the ritual of a well-made drink in a room that means something. Come as a guest. Leave as a regular.

Our namesake

Aged rye whiskey, house-made walnut bitters, a single perfect cherry, and just enough sweet vermouth to make you ask for another. Served over a single large cube, always.

No. 02

Midnight Sour

Bourbon, fresh lemon, honey syrup, egg white. Silky, bright, and dangerously easy to drink.

No. 03

Olive Street Martini

House-infused olive brine, Grey Goose, a whisper of dry vermouth. Stirred until perfectly cold.

No. 04

Lake Bluff Mai Tai

Aged Jamaican rum, fresh lime, orgeat, orange curaçao. Our nod to the tropics — done right, no shortcuts.

No. 05

Jungle Bird

Blackstrap rum, Campari, fresh pineapple, lime, and simple syrup. Bitter, tropical, and completely addictive.

No. 06

Zombie (House Rules)

Three rums, apricot liqueur, falernum, fresh citrus, Angostura. One per guest. No exceptions. You'll understand why.

No. 07

Paper Plane

Equal parts Aperol, Amaro Nonino, bourbon, and lemon juice. A modern classic, perfectly balanced.

It doesn't look like a bar from the outside. That's entirely the point.
